Gangtok | Darjeeling – 4 Nights / 5 Days
  • ITINERARY AND OTHER INFORMTION
    The Journey Begins Now!
     
    DAY 01 : ARRIVAL Gangtok
    Meet & greet on arrival at Bagdogra Airport / NJP Railway Station & transfer to Gangtok. On arrival Check-into hotel & rest of the day at leisure. Overnight stay in Gangtok.
     
    DAY 02 : Gangtok - Tsomgo Lake
    After breakfast excursion to Tsomgo Lake and Baba Mandir. Optional visit to Nathula Pass (subject to permit). Evening return to Gangtok. Overnight stay in Gangtok.
     
    DAY 03 : Gangtok Sightseeing
    After breakfast Gangtok city tour including Rumtek Monastery, Enchey Monastery, Institute of Tibetology, Flower Exhibition Centre, and local markets. Overnight stay in Gangtok.
     
    DAY 04 : Gangtok - Darjeeling
    After breakfast proceed to Darjeeling. En route visit Namchi and Ravangla (optional). Check into hotel in Darjeeling. Evening visit to Mall Road. Overnight stay in Darjeeling.
     
    DAY 05 : Darjeeling DEPARTURE
    Early morning visit to Tiger Hill for sunrise over Kanchenjunga. Visit Ghoom Monastery and Darjeeling Tea Gardens. Transfer to Bagdogra Airport/NJP Station for departure.

    • Air fare / Train fare / Bus fare.
