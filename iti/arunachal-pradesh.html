Arunachal Pradesh

Land of Dawn-Lit Mountains
Itanagar | Tawang – 6 Nights / 7 Days
  • ITINERARY AND OTHER INFORMTION
    The Journey Begins Now!
     
    DAY 01 : ARRIVAL Guwahati - Itanagar
    Meet & greet on arrival at Guwahati Airport / Railway Station & proceed to Itanagar. Check-into hotel & rest of the day at leisure. Overnight stay in Itanagar.
     
    DAY 02 : Itanagar Sightseeing
    After breakfast visit Itanagar Fort, Ganga Lake, Jawaharlal Nehru State Museum, and local markets. Overnight stay in Itanagar.
     
    DAY 03 : Itanagar - Bomdila
    After breakfast proceed to Bomdila. Visit Bomdila Monastery and Apple Orchards. Overnight stay in Bomdila.
     
    DAY 04 : Bomdila - Tawang
    After breakfast proceed to Tawang via Sela Pass. Check into hotel. Evening visit to Tawang War Memorial. Overnight stay in Tawang.
     
    DAY 05 : Tawang Sightseeing
    After breakfast full day Tawang sightseeing including Tawang Monastery, Madhuri Lake, and local villages. Overnight stay in Tawang.
     
    DAY 06 : Tawang - Guwahati
    After breakfast proceed back to Guwahati. Overnight stay in Guwahati.
     
    DAY 07 : Guwahati DEPARTURE
    After breakfast transfer to Airport/Railway Station for departure.
