Kuala Lumpur | Genting Highlands | Langkawi | Penang – 6 Nights / 7 Days
  • ITINERARY AND OTHER INFORMTION
    Malaysian Adventure!
     
    DAY 01 : ARRIVAL Kuala Lumpur
    Meet & greet on arrival at Kuala Lumpur Airport & transfer to hotel. Evening visit to Petronas Twin Towers and KLCC Park. Overnight stay in Kuala Lumpur.
     
    DAY 02 : Kuala Lumpur - Genting Highlands
    After breakfast proceed to Genting Highlands. Enjoy cable car ride and theme park. Evening at casino and entertainment. Overnight stay in Genting.
     
    DAY 03 : Genting - Kuala Lumpur
    After breakfast return to Kuala Lumpur. City tour including Batu Caves, National Mosque, and Central Market. Overnight stay in Kuala Lumpur.
     
    DAY 04 : Kuala Lumpur - Langkawi
    After breakfast fly to Langkawi. Check into beach resort. Evening at Pantai Cenang Beach. Overnight stay in Langkawi.
     
    DAY 05 : Langkawi
    After breakfast island hopping tour - Eagle Watching, Pregnant Maiden Lake, and Beras Basah Island. Evening cable car ride. Overnight stay in Langkawi.
     
    DAY 06 : Langkawi - Penang
    After breakfast fly to Penang. Visit Georgetown UNESCO World Heritage Site and Penang Hill. Overnight stay in Penang.
     
    DAY 07 : Penang DEPARTURE
    After breakfast transfer to Airport for departure.
