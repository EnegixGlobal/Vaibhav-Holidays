Bhutan

Land of Thunder Dragon
Thimphu | Paro | Punakha | Wangdue – 5 Nights / 6 Days
  • ITINERARY AND OTHER INFORMTION
    Last Shangri-La!
     
    DAY 01 : ARRIVAL Paro - Thimphu
    Meet & greet on arrival at Paro Airport & transfer to Thimphu. Visit Tashichho Dzong and Memorial Chorten. Overnight stay in Thimphu.
     
    DAY 02 : Thimphu
    After breakfast visit Buddha Dordenma, National Library, and weekend market. Visit Textile Museum and Folk Heritage Museum. Overnight stay in Thimphu.
     
    DAY 03 : Thimphu - Punakha
    After breakfast proceed to Punakha via Dochula Pass. Visit Punakha Dzong and Chimi Lhakhang. Overnight stay in Punakha.
     
    DAY 04 : Punakha - Wangdue
    After breakfast proceed to Wangdue. Visit Wangdue Dzong and local villages. Evening at leisure. Overnight stay in Wangdue.
     
    DAY 05 : Wangdue - Paro
    After breakfast return to Paro. Visit Rinpung Dzong and National Museum. Overnight stay in Paro.
     
    DAY 06 : Paro - Tiger's Nest - DEPARTURE
    Early morning hike to Tiger's Nest Monastery. Return to hotel and transfer to Airport for departure.
