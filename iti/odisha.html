Bhubaneswar | Puri | Konark – 3 Nights / 4 Days
  • ITINERARY AND OTHER INFORMTION
    The Journey Begins Now!
     
    DAY 01 : ARRIVAL Bhubaneswar
    Meet & greet on arrival at Bhubaneswar Airport / Railway Station & transfer to hotel. On arrival Check-into hotel & rest of the day at leisure. Overnight stay in Bhubaneswar.
     
    DAY 02 : Bhubaneswar - Puri
    After breakfast proceed to Puri via Konark. Visit Konark Sun Temple en route. Check into hotel in Puri. Evening visit to Jagannath Temple and Puri Beach. Overnight stay in Puri.
     
    DAY 03 : Puri Sightseeing
    After breakfast full day sightseeing of Puri including Jagannath Temple, Gundicha Temple, Puri Beach, Chilika Lake (optional). Evening free for shopping. Overnight stay in Puri.
     
    DAY 04 : Puri - Bhubaneswar DEPARTURE
    After breakfast Check-out from hotel and proceed to Bhubaneswar. City tour including Lingaraj Temple, Khandagiri and Udayagiri Caves. Transfer to Airport/Railway Station for departure.
