Kathmandu | Pokhara | Chitwan – 5 Nights / 6 Days
  • ITINERARY AND OTHER INFORMTION
    Himalayan Adventure!
     
    DAY 01 : ARRIVAL Kathmandu
    Meet & greet on arrival at Kathmandu Airport & transfer to hotel. Evening visit to Thamel market. Overnight stay in Kathmandu.
     
    DAY 02 : Kathmandu Sightseeing
    After breakfast visit Pashupatinath Temple, Boudhanath Stupa, and Swayambhunath (Monkey Temple). Visit Kathmandu Durbar Square. Overnight stay in Kathmandu.
     
    DAY 03 : Kathmandu - Pokhara
    After breakfast drive to Pokhara. Visit Phewa Lake and Tal Barahi Temple. Evening at leisure. Overnight stay in Pokhara.
     
    DAY 04 : Pokhara
    Early morning visit to Sarangkot for sunrise view of Himalayas. Visit Devi's Fall, Gupteshwor Cave, and International Mountain Museum. Overnight stay in Pokhara.
     
    DAY 05 : Pokhara - Chitwan
    After breakfast proceed to Chitwan National Park. Evening cultural program. Overnight stay in Chitwan.
     
    DAY 06 : Chitwan - Kathmandu DEPARTURE
    Early morning jungle safari and elephant ride. Return to Kathmandu. Transfer to Airport for departure.
