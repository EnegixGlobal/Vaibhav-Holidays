Shimla | Manali – 5 Nights / 6 Days
  • ITINERARY AND OTHER INFORMTION
    The Journey Begins Now!
     
    DAY 01 : ARRIVAL Shimla
    Meet & greet on arrival at Shimla / Chandigarh & transfer to hotel in Shimla. On arrival Check-into hotel & rest of the day at leisure. Evening walk on Mall Road. Overnight stay in Shimla.
     
    DAY 02 : Shimla Sightseeing
    After breakfast Shimla local sightseeing including Vice Regal Lodge, Christ Church, The Ridge, Scandal Point, and Jakhu Temple. Evening shopping at Mall Road. Overnight stay in Shimla.
     
    DAY 03 : Shimla - Manali
    After breakfast proceed to Manali. En route visit Kullu Valley and Pandoh Dam. Check into hotel in Manali. Evening free for leisure. Overnight stay in Manali.
     
    DAY 04 : Manali - Solang Valley
    After breakfast excursion to Solang Valley for adventure activities like paragliding, zorbing, and skiing (seasonal). Visit Hadimba Temple and Vashisht Hot Springs. Overnight stay in Manali.
     
    DAY 05 : Manali - Rohtang Pass
    After breakfast full day excursion to Rohtang Pass (subject to weather conditions). Enjoy snow activities and scenic beauty. Return to Manali. Overnight stay in Manali.
     
    DAY 06 : Manali DEPARTURE
    After breakfast Check-out from hotel and transfer to Chandigarh Airport/Railway Station for departure.
     
    COST INCLUDES :
    • Hotel Category – 3 Star
    • Meal Plan – CP (Only Breakfast)
    • No. of vehicle – Exclusive private AC vehicle
    • 01 Liter mineral water bottle (01 Pc. per person per day)
    • Parking charges
    • GST
    • All permit fees & hotel taxes (as per itinerary).
     
    COST EXCLUDES:
    • Air fare / Train fare / Bus fare.
    • Any other meals not mentioned in the inclusions.
    • Personal Expenses such as Laundry, Telephone calls, Tips & Gratuity.
    • Additional sightseeing or extra usage of vehicle, other than mentioned in the itinerary.
    • Adventure activity charges, Cable car, Pony ride charges.
    • Rohtang Pass permit fees and local taxi charges.
    • Any cost arising due to natural calamities like, landslides, road blockage, political disturbances, etc.
    • Travel Insurances.
    • Anything which is not include in the inclusion.
     
    NOTE :
    1. Rohtang Pass visit is subject to weather conditions and NGT permit availability.
    2. Itinerary may change, depending upon the situation. Please cooperate in case of unavoidable circumstances like – natural calamities, strikes, sudden technical faults of vehicles, accident, theft, robbery etc.
