Agatti | Bangaram | Kavaratti – 4 Nights / 5 Days
  • ITINERARY AND OTHER INFORMTION
    Tropical Bliss!
     
    DAY 01 : ARRIVAL Agatti
    Meet & greet on arrival at Agatti Airport & transfer to resort. Evening at pristine Agatti Beach. Overnight stay in Agatti.
     
    DAY 02 : Agatti - Bangaram
    After breakfast boat transfer to Bangaram Island. Check into resort. Enjoy snorkeling and coral viewing. Overnight stay in Bangaram.
     
    DAY 03 : Bangaram Island
    Full day at leisure. Enjoy water sports - deep sea fishing, kayaking, scuba diving. Evening sunset cruise. Overnight stay in Bangaram.
     
    DAY 04 : Bangaram - Kavaratti
    After breakfast proceed to Kavaratti. Visit Marine Aquarium and local cultural sites. Overnight stay in Kavaratti.
     
    DAY 05 : Kavaratti - Agatti DEPARTURE
    After breakfast return to Agatti. Transfer to Airport for departure.
