Chennai | Bangalore | Mysore | Ooty – 6 Nights / 7 Days
  • ITINERARY AND OTHER INFORMTION
    Discover the Heritage of South India!
     
    DAY 01 : ARRIVAL Chennai
    Meet & greet on arrival at Chennai Airport / Railway Station & transfer to hotel. Evening visit to Marina Beach and local markets. Overnight stay in Chennai.
     
    DAY 02 : Chennai Sightseeing
    After breakfast Chennai city tour including Kapaleeshwar Temple, San Thome Cathedral, Fort St. George, and Government Museum. Overnight stay in Chennai.
     
    DAY 03 : Chennai - Bangalore
    After breakfast proceed to Bangalore. En route visit Kanchipuram temples (optional). Check into hotel. Evening visit to MG Road and Brigade Road. Overnight stay in Bangalore.
     
    DAY 04 : Bangalore - Mysore
    After breakfast proceed to Mysore. Visit Mysore Palace, Chamundi Hills, and St. Philomena's Church. Overnight stay in Mysore.
     
    DAY 05 : Mysore - Ooty
    After breakfast proceed to Ooty, the Queen of Hill Stations. Check into hotel. Evening visit to Ooty Lake. Overnight stay in Ooty.
     
    DAY 06 : Ooty Sightseeing
    After breakfast visit Botanical Gardens, Doddabetta Peak, Tea Gardens, and enjoy Toy Train ride. Overnight stay in Ooty.
     
    DAY 07 : Ooty - Bangalore DEPARTURE
    After breakfast return to Bangalore. Transfer to Airport/Railway Station for departure.
