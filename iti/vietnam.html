Ho Chi Minh | Hanoi | Halong Bay | Hoi An – 6 Nights / 7 Days
  • ITINERARY AND OTHER INFORMTION
    Vietnamese Adventure!
     
    DAY 01 : ARRIVAL Ho Chi Minh City
    Meet & greet on arrival at Ho Chi Minh Airport & transfer to hotel. Evening visit to Ben Thanh Market and Nguyen Hue Walking Street. Overnight stay in Ho Chi Minh.
     
    DAY 02 : Ho Chi Minh - Cu Chi Tunnels
    After breakfast visit Cu Chi Tunnels and learn about Vietnam War history. Visit Reunification Palace and War Remnants Museum. Overnight stay in Ho Chi Minh.
     
    DAY 03 : Ho Chi Minh - Hanoi
    After breakfast fly to Hanoi. Visit Hoan Kiem Lake and Temple of Literature. Evening at Old Quarter. Overnight stay in Hanoi.
     
    DAY 04 : Hanoi - Halong Bay
    After breakfast drive to Halong Bay. Board traditional junk boat. Cruise through limestone karsts and emerald waters. Overnight on boat.
     
    DAY 05 : Halong Bay - Hoi An
    After breakfast return to Hanoi and fly to Da Nang. Transfer to Hoi An. Evening walk through Ancient Town. Overnight stay in Hoi An.
     
    DAY 06 : Hoi An
    After breakfast visit My Son Sanctuary. Afternoon cooking class and bike tour through countryside. Evening at Japanese Covered Bridge. Overnight stay in Hoi An.
     
    DAY 07 : Hoi An - Da Nang DEPARTURE
    After breakfast transfer to Da Nang Airport for departure.
