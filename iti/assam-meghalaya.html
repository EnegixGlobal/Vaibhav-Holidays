Assam & Meghalaya

Land of Blue Hills and Red Rivers
Guwahati | Shillong | Cherrapunji – 5 Nights / 6 Days
  • ITINERARY AND OTHER INFORMTION
    The Journey Begins Now!
     
    DAY 01 : ARRIVAL Guwahati
    Meet & greet on arrival at Guwahati Airport / Railway Station & transfer to hotel. On arrival Check-into hotel. Evening visit to Kamakhya Temple. Overnight stay in Guwahati.
     
    DAY 02 : Guwahati - Shillong
    After breakfast proceed to Shillong, the Scotland of the East. En route visit Umiam Lake. Check into hotel in Shillong. Evening visit to Police Bazaar. Overnight stay in Shillong.
     
    DAY 03 : Shillong - Cherrapunji
    After breakfast full day excursion to Cherrapunji. Visit Nohkalikai Falls, Seven Sisters Falls, Mawsmai Cave, and Eco Park. Return to Shillong in evening. Overnight stay in Shillong.
     
    DAY 04 : Shillong Sightseeing
    After breakfast Shillong city tour including Elephant Falls, Shillong Peak, Lady Hydari Park, Golf Course, and local markets. Overnight stay in Shillong.
     
    DAY 05 : Shillong - Guwahati
    After breakfast return to Guwahati. Visit Assam State Museum, Umananda Temple, and River Cruise on Brahmaputra (optional). Overnight stay in Guwahati.
     
    DAY 06 : Guwahati DEPARTURE
    After breakfast Check-out from hotel and transfer to Airport/Railway Station for departure.
