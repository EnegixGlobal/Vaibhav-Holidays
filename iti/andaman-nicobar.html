Andaman and Nicobar

Island Paradise
Port Blair | Havelock | Neil Island – 5 Nights / 6 Days
  • ITINERARY AND OTHER INFORMTION
    Paradise Found!
     
    DAY 01 : ARRIVAL Port Blair
    Meet & greet on arrival at Port Blair Airport & transfer to hotel. Evening visit to Corbyn's Cove Beach and Light & Sound Show at Cellular Jail. Overnight stay in Port Blair.
     
    DAY 02 : Port Blair - Havelock
    After breakfast board ferry to Havelock Island. Check into resort. Visit Radhanagar Beach. Overnight stay in Havelock.
     
    DAY 03 : Havelock Island
    After breakfast enjoy water sports at Elephant Beach - snorkeling, sea walking. Evening at leisure. Overnight stay in Havelock.
     
    DAY 04 : Havelock - Neil Island
    After breakfast proceed to Neil Island. Visit Bharatpur Beach and Laxmanpur Beach. Overnight stay in Neil Island.
     
    DAY 05 : Neil Island - Port Blair
    After breakfast return to Port Blair. Visit Chatham Saw Mill and Anthropological Museum. Overnight stay in Port Blair.
     
    DAY 06 : Port Blair DEPARTURE
    After breakfast transfer to Airport for departure.
