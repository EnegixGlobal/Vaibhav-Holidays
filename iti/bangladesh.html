Bangladesh

Land of Rivers
Dhaka | Cox's Bazar | Sylhet | Sundarbans – 5 Nights / 6 Days
  • ITINERARY AND OTHER INFORMTION
    Bengal Heritage!
     
    DAY 01 : ARRIVAL Dhaka
    Meet & greet on arrival at Dhaka Airport & transfer to hotel. Evening visit to Lalbagh Fort and Ahsan Manzil. Overnight stay in Dhaka.
     
    DAY 02 : Dhaka - Cox's Bazar
    After breakfast fly to Cox's Bazar. Check into hotel. Evening at Cox's Bazar Beach - world's longest natural beach. Overnight stay in Cox's Bazar.
     
    DAY 03 : Cox's Bazar
    After breakfast visit Himchari National Park and Inani Beach. Sunset at Laboni Beach. Overnight stay in Cox's Bazar.
     
    DAY 04 : Cox's Bazar - Dhaka - Sylhet
    After breakfast fly back to Dhaka and connect to Sylhet. Visit Shrine of Hazrat Shah Jalal. Overnight stay in Sylhet.
     
    DAY 05 : Sylhet - Sundarbans
    After breakfast proceed to Sundarbans National Park. Boat safari in mangrove forests. Overnight stay in Sundarbans.
     
    DAY 06 : Sundarbans - Dhaka DEPARTURE
    Early morning wildlife safari. Return to Dhaka. Transfer to Airport for departure.
