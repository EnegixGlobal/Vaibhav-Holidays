    Explore the Royal Heritage!
     
    DAY 01 : ARRIVAL Jaipur
    Meet & greet on arrival at Jaipur Airport/Railway Station & transfer to hotel. Evening visit to local markets. Overnight stay in Jaipur.
     
    DAY 02 : Jaipur Sightseeing
    After breakfast visit Amber Fort, City Palace, Jantar Mantar, and Hawa Mahal. Evening free for shopping. Overnight stay in Jaipur.
     
    DAY 03 : Jaipur - Jodhpur
    After breakfast proceed to Jodhpur. En route visit Pushkar (optional). Check into hotel. Evening visit to Clock Tower Market. Overnight stay in Jodhpur.
     
    DAY 04 : Jodhpur - Udaipur
    After breakfast visit Mehrangarh Fort and Jaswant Thada. Proceed to Udaipur. Check into hotel. Overnight stay in Udaipur.
     
    DAY 05 : Udaipur Sightseeing
    After breakfast visit City Palace, Lake Pichola boat ride, Jagdish Temple, and Saheliyon ki Bari. Overnight stay in Udaipur.
     
    DAY 06 : Udaipur - Jaipur
    After breakfast return to Jaipur. Visit Chittorgarh Fort en route (optional). Overnight stay in Jaipur.
     
    DAY 07 : Jaipur DEPARTURE
    After breakfast transfer to Airport/Railway Station for departure.
