Colombo | Kandy | Nuwara Eliya | Bentota – 6 Nights / 7 Days
  • ITINERARY AND OTHER INFORMTION
    Island Paradise!
     
    DAY 01 : ARRIVAL Colombo
    Meet & greet on arrival at Colombo Airport & transfer to hotel. Evening city tour including Independence Square and Galle Face Green. Overnight stay in Colombo.
     
    DAY 02 : Colombo - Kandy
    After breakfast proceed to Kandy. Visit Pinnawala Elephant Orphanage en route. Visit Temple of Tooth Relic and Kandy Lake. Overnight stay in Kandy.
     
    DAY 03 : Kandy - Nuwara Eliya
    After breakfast proceed to Nuwara Eliya. Visit tea plantations and tea factory. Evening at Gregory Lake. Overnight stay in Nuwara Eliya.
     
    DAY 04 : Nuwara Eliya
    After breakfast visit Hakgala Botanical Gardens and Seetha Amman Temple. Afternoon at leisure. Overnight stay in Nuwara Eliya.
     
    DAY 05 : Nuwara Eliya - Bentota
    After breakfast proceed to Bentota. Check into beach resort. Evening at leisure on beach. Overnight stay in Bentota.
     
    DAY 06 : Bentota
    Full day at leisure. Enjoy water sports, Ayurvedic spa treatments, or explore local markets. Overnight stay in Bentota.
     
    DAY 07 : Bentota - Colombo DEPARTURE
    After breakfast return to Colombo. Transfer to Airport for departure.
