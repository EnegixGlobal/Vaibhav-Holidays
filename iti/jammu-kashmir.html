Jammu & Kashmir

Paradise on Earth
Srinagar | Pahalgam | Gulmarg – 5 Nights / 6 Days
  • ITINERARY AND OTHER INFORMTION
    The Journey Begins Now!
     
    DAY 01 : ARRIVAL Srinagar
    Meet & greet on arrival at Srinagar Airport & transfer to houseboat/hotel. Check-into accommodation. Evening visit to local markets and Dal Lake. Overnight stay in Srinagar.
     
    DAY 02 : Srinagar Sightseeing
    After breakfast Srinagar city tour including Mughal Gardens (Nishat, Shalimar, Chashme Shahi), Shankaracharya Temple, and Dal Lake Shikara ride. Overnight stay in Srinagar.
     
    DAY 03 : Srinagar - Pahalgam
    After breakfast proceed to Pahalgam, the Valley of Shepherds. En route visit Saffron fields and Awantipora ruins. Check into hotel. Evening leisure walk along Lidder River. Overnight stay in Pahalgam.
     
    DAY 04 : Pahalgam - Gulmarg
    After breakfast proceed to Gulmarg, the Meadow of Flowers. Check into hotel. Enjoy Gondola cable car ride (Phase 1 & 2). Evening free for leisure. Overnight stay in Gulmarg.
     
    DAY 05 : Gulmarg - Srinagar
    After breakfast return to Srinagar. Visit Hazratbal Shrine and local handicraft centers. Last minute shopping. Overnight stay in Srinagar.
     
    DAY 06 : Srinagar DEPARTURE
    After breakfast transfer to Srinagar Airport for departure.
