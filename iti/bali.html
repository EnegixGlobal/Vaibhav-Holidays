Kuta | Ubud | Sanur | Nusa Dua – 5 Nights / 6 Days
  • ITINERARY AND OTHER INFORMTION
    Tropical Paradise!
     
    DAY 01 : ARRIVAL Kuta
    Meet & greet on arrival at Denpasar Airport & transfer to Kuta hotel. Evening at Kuta Beach and sunset at Tanah Lot Temple. Overnight stay in Kuta.
     
    DAY 02 : Kuta - Ubud
    After breakfast proceed to Ubud. Visit Tegallalang Rice Terraces and Sacred Monkey Forest. Check into resort. Overnight stay in Ubud.
     
    DAY 03 : Ubud
    After breakfast visit traditional art villages - Mas (wood carving), Celuk (silver), Batuan (painting). Visit Ubud Palace and Traditional Market. Overnight stay in Ubud.
     
    DAY 04 : Ubud - Sanur
    After breakfast proceed to Sanur. Visit Besakih Temple (Mother Temple). Check into beach resort. Evening at Sanur Beach. Overnight stay in Sanur.
     
    DAY 05 : Sanur - Nusa Dua
    After breakfast proceed to Nusa Dua. Visit Uluwatu Temple and Kecak Fire Dance. Check into luxury resort. Overnight stay in Nusa Dua.
     
    DAY 06 : Nusa Dua DEPARTURE
    After breakfast at leisure until transfer to Airport for departure.
