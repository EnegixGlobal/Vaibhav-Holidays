Leh | Nubra Valley | Pangong Lake – 5 Nights / 6 Days
  • ITINERARY AND OTHER INFORMTION
    Experience the Roof of the World!
     
    DAY 01 : ARRIVAL Leh
    Meet & greet on arrival at Leh Airport & transfer to hotel. Complete rest for acclimatization. Evening visit to Leh Market and Shanti Stupa. Overnight stay in Leh.
     
    DAY 02 : Leh Sightseeing
    After breakfast visit Thiksey Monastery, Hemis Monastery, and Shey Palace. Evening visit to Hall of Fame Museum. Overnight stay in Leh.
     
    DAY 03 : Leh - Nubra Valley
    After breakfast proceed to Nubra Valley via Khardung La Pass (world's highest motorable road). Visit Diskit Monastery and enjoy Camel Safari in Hunder Sand Dunes. Overnight stay in Nubra Valley.
     
    DAY 04 : Nubra Valley - Pangong Lake
    After breakfast proceed to Pangong Lake via Shyok River route. Enjoy the changing colors of the famous lake. Overnight stay near Pangong Lake.
     
    DAY 05 : Pangong Lake - Leh
    After breakfast return to Leh via Chang La Pass. En route visit Changla Cafe (world's highest cafe). Evening free for shopping. Overnight stay in Leh.
     
    DAY 06 : Leh DEPARTURE
    After breakfast transfer to Leh Airport for departure.
